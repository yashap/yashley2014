Why Carpool?

If you're taking your car on the ferry, the main cost is the cost of the car itself (see the Wedding Weekend page for more detials). The fee for the car itself is $102.50 there-and-back, but if you split that 4 ways, it's a much more reasonable $25 each. Plus, carpooling is fun!

How to Organize Rides

There's a little discussion board below. If you have a spare seat (or seats), or if you need a ride, let everyone know, and work out a carpool! To post, just sign in with your Facebook, Twitter or Google account, or create a new account with Disqus (the software powering this discussion board).
