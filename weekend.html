How to get there

If you're coming from Vancouver, drive to the Horseshoe Bay Ferry terminal, and catch a ferry to Nanaimo. You can check out their website for departure times, prices and reservations, but we'd suggest the 2:10 pm ferry on the way there (June 6th), and either the 12:50 pm or 3:10 pm ferry on the way back (June 8th).

The ferry is $51.25 each way for a vehicle, plus $12.50 each way per passenger (over 12 years old), and a $15 fee if you want to reserve your spot. It's obviously way cheaper if you split the car price, so check out our carpool page to organize some ride sharing!

For driving directions from Nanaimo, Google Maps will do a better job describing than we can! Long story short, you take the BC-19 N for about 160 km, until you're just past Campbell River. Then it's a right on McDonald Rd., and the first left onto Discovery Dr. Dolphin's Resort is at 4125 Discover Dr. (on your right).

The itinerary

Show up any time from ~4pm onwards on Friday June 6th. We'll have a casual dinner starting around 7pm, and have a generally relaxed night. There will be drinks, swimming in the ocean if you like, and warming up in hot tubs too! Friday night will be an informal event, and for the whole weekend food and drinks are on us!

Saturday is the day of the wedding itself, so dress fancy. We'll start the day off with some brunch, then head down to the beach for the wedding ceremony. Or, if it's raining, we'll have the ceremony in the main reception hall. After that it's on to dinner and dancing at night!

On Sunday, we'll all have breakfast together, then head back to the ferries.

Booking your room

We'll all be sleeping in one of two places - at cabins in Dolphin's Resort itself, or at Painter's Lodge, just 800m down the road.

In the invite you receive in the mail, you should get details about where we've saved a room for you (either Dolphin's or Painter's), and who to call to book! At Dolphin's Resort it's $170 per person for the weekend (both nights), and at Painter's Lodge prices vary.
